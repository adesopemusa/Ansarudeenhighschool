Our History & Background

Ansarudeen Islamic High School was established in 1982 at Otun-Ekiti, Moba Local Government Area, Ekiti State. Since its inception, the school has remained committed to raising God-fearing and well-educated leaders of tomorrow.

With a strong foundation in both academic excellence and moral discipline, the school provides a balanced education that prepares students for success in life. Over the years, it has grown to become a trusted institution within the community, recognized for its dedication to quality teaching and learning.

One of the core values of Ansarudeen Islamic High School is affordability. By maintaining school fees that are within reach for parents, the institution ensures that quality education is accessible to all, regardless of financial background.

Through its vision and mission, the school continues to nurture students who excel not only in academics but also in character, leadership, and service to humanity.
